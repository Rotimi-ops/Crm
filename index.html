Key Challenges and Mitigation Strategies

Integration with Existing Systems: Comprehensive compatibility testing and validation.

User Adoption and Training: Develop training materials and provide ongoing support.

Data Security and Privacy: Implement data encryption, access controls, and audits.

Customisation and Scalability: Modular architecture for customization and scalability.

Risk Management and Mitigation

Budget Overruns: Monitor expenses, implement cost-control measures.

Delays in Development: Include buffer time, review progress.

Inadequate User Training: Allocate resources, use diverse training methods.

System Downtime During Migration: Phased rollout, thorough testing, and rollback plan.
